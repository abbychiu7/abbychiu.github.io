My name is Abby Chiu and I am a Programmer, Editor, and Graphic Designer. My interest in graphic design sprouted when my family established a business, and I recognized the challenges they faced in securing a graphic designer for our logo. Witnessing their struggle, I volunteered to lend my skills. This marked the inception of my passion for graphic design, as I found joy in transforming concepts into visual representations. The logo featured below stands as evidence of my commitment and creative input, contributing to the success of our family's business. Conversely, my fondness for video editing took root during our family travels. Captivated by the beauty of each moment, I felt compelled to capture and immortalize these experiences. Keen to share these memories, I began offering video editing services to enhance and breathe life into the footage. The link provided below will direct you to one of my edited videos, showcasing my dedication to visual storytelling. Feel free to explore the world through my lens.
